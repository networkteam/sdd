Framework naming exploration produced 'Graiki' as the leading candidate. Invented word encoding the framework's core identity: Gr(aph) + AI + ki. Also carries echoes of 'haiku' (small, precise, structured) and Graikoi (Greek root of structured thought). CLI would be gki. Runner-up 'Sigdis' (signal + decision, Norse roots) was considered but sounds harsh and carries an unfortunate 'diss' undertone. Graiki is novel, unclaimed, approachable without being frivolous — fits a lightweight process framework. No decision yet — letting it breathe.
