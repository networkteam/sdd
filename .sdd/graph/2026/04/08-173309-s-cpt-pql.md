No mechanism for attaching supporting material to graph entries. Longer decisions with research, background, or diagrams need a way to reference external files that are stored alongside the entry and are immutable like the entries themselves. Proposed convention: a directory named after the entry ID (e.g. 20260408-171831-d-cpt-axa/) containing attachment files, co-located in the graph directory.
