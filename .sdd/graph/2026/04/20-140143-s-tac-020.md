## Dispatch overhaul

`selectCheckType` became kind-aware:
- Completion records (type=action legacy, or signal with kind=done) + closes ‚Üí closing_done if any target is a decision, else short_loop
- Signal with kind=fact/insight + closes ‚Üí dissolution (non-question targets flagged by unusual_close partial)
- Decision with kind=aspiration (any refs shape) ‚Üí aspiration_capture
- Decision + closes ‚Üí closing_decision
- Decision, no closes ‚Üí decision_refs
- Signal otherwise ‚Üí signal_capture
- Supersedes always takes priority
