Conversation mining as automatic signal generation. Mine session transcripts to produce low-confidence signals with no human participant. These flow through normal catch-up and grooming for human review — dialogue-before-capture is preserved because mining produces candidates, not committed entries. Each session must be mined at most once (idempotency). Consistent with story.md's vision of agent-contributed signals (analytics agents, review agents). Automatic signals should show low confidence and that no human participated, making their provisional status visible in catch-up.
