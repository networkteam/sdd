Pre-flight rejects signals containing solution-space thinking as 'crossing into decision territory.' Signals expand dialogue by bringing observations and potential directions as material for discussion. Decisions resolve dialogue into commitments — committing to a direction, whether that's a one-line choice or a detailed plan. The validation should test whether the entry prescribes a commitment (decision) or explores material for dialogue (signal), not whether it mentions a solution.
