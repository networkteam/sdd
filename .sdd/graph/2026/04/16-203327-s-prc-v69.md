CLI sdd status and sdd list output lacks kind and participants per line, forcing any consumer needing those facts (sub-skills like /sdd-catchup, /sdd-explore, /sdd-groom; ad-hoc agent queries; humans reading lines out of context) to fall back on per-entry sdd show. Wasted round-trips, wasted LLM budget when sub-skills do the lookup, silent data loss when they don't. Agents read these outputs far more than humans; the shape should be agent-first with readability second. Line width is not the constraint; uniform presence of structurally relevant fields is. Related: s-prc-omw (participant naming inconsistency shares the remediation path for the participants field) and s-prc-hpa (same category — push deterministic facts out of the LLM layer).
