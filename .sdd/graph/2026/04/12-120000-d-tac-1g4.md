Improve pre-flight accuracy by flowing dialogue context into entries and teaching the validator to respect it. Two changes: (1) Update the Always dialogue before capturing section in SKILL.md to guide including substantive dialogue conclusions (trade-offs discussed, alternatives rejected, reasoning for the closure) in the entry description — the user confirms at play-back, providing grounding without a separate attachment. (2) Update pre-flight prompt templates to recognize dialogue-sourced reasoning in the entry text as human-confirmed context. The validator should check structural completeness (missing refs, uncovered requirements, no evidence) but not re-litigate rationale that the entry describes as dialogued and confirmed.
