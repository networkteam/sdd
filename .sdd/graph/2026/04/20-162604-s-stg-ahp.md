External talk frames SDD's core mechanism: deliberate friction is the steering force in agent workflows — autonomous loops must be slowed at the points where dialogue is required, and that is where judgment and experience lives. "The Friction Is Your Judgment" (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Zcw_sVF6hU) by Armin Ronacher & Cristina Poncela Cubeiro (Earendil) names what SDD's pre-capture dialogue and pre-flight already do: not process overhead, but the structural mechanism for inserting human judgment into autonomous execution.
