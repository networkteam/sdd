Pre-flight validation does not flag unrelated references. An entry that refs a contract it merely followed rather than builds on should be caught. Refs means builds-on or depends-on, not this rule applied during my work. Contracts apply automatically via the active contracts context. Explicit refs should only exist when the entry genuinely extends or depends on the referenced entry. Calibration should treat unrelated refs as high severity to prevent the graph from accumulating misleading relationships.
