Improve skill capture playbook with explicit attachment assessment and session-finishing moves. The capture dialogue must include a step where the agent assesses whether the conversation produced material worth preserving as an attachment — reasoning chains, design alternatives, rejected approaches, draft plans. Add playbook moves for concluding capture scenarios: after design dialogue (default to attachment with draft plan), after evaluation (attach findings), after exploration (attach context synthesis). The agent proposes the attachment content as part of the play-back-what-you-would-capture step, not as an afterthought.
