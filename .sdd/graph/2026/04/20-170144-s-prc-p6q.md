The SDD skill lacks a "mine" playbook move for extracting insights from external sources — transcripts, URLs, documents — through guided dialogue. This session demonstrated the pattern with a podcast transcript: load the source, identify touchpoints with the graph, dialogue to filter for relevance, capture selected insights with source URL and verbatim quotes attached. Without a named move the pattern stays ad-hoc and undiscoverable.
