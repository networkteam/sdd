Insight signals appear in Open Signals in `sdd status` but carry no closure gate — they're observational records, not actionable gaps. Status should surface insights separately (e.g. 'Recent Insights') rather than mixing them into the actionable Open Signals view, parallel to how Recent Done Signals was split out from Open Signals per s-tac-vk7.
