Mining external conversations (Slack, customer calls, non-SDD coding sessions, design discussions) as a signal source for the graph. Distinct from mining SDD sessions (s-cpt-bn1): external conversations never touch the graph at all, so there is no pre-flight or dialogue-before-capture to catch what is missed. In the story.md pattern, Jun hears from customers at the counter, Mara gets phone calls, analytics agents read engagement data — these are external inputs that currently require someone to manually recognize and capture them. Mining could surface candidates for human review. This is also MemPalace's core use case — their entire system is built around mining conversation exports and making them findable.
