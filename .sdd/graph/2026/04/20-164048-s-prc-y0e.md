Pre-flight reports `missing-explicit-kind` as a medium finding even when the entry carries an explicit `kind` field — confirmed false positive in two sessions (s-stg-ljq and another). The LLM-based field-presence check (s-prc-hpa) cannot reliably detect metadata fields that are present, producing noise that erodes trust in pre-flight findings.
