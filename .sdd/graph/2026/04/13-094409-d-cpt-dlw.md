Fork resonance into a dedicated SDD repo to make SDD a distributable, self-contained project. Current resonance scope is the retro-cycle harness (create-spec, task-retro, spec-retro, meta-retro, meta-retro-followup); SDD has grown into something separate and shouldn't remain a subdirectory of it. The split is about repo hygiene and distribution readiness, not a hard break — SDD continues its trajectory, resonance keeps its original harness identity. Scope: repo structure, folder layout, README written for external evaluators, getting-started docs. Enables s-stg-qg0 (adoption paths) by giving SDD a clean home that can be evaluated in other projects without carrying the resonance harness as baggage. Alternatives considered: renaming resonance to sdd (loses the harness identity and conflates two projects), keeping as subdirectory (blocks external distribution and clouds positioning). Open questions live in the attached plan: exact folder structure, what moves vs stays, git history preservation, relationship to distribution story. See [fork plan](./13-094409-d-cpt-dlw/plan.md) for details.
