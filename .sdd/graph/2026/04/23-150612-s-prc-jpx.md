Sharpening the /sdd skill's conflict-predicted bullet from passive ("let them resolve manually") to active: name paths, offer to drive `git pull --rebase` together, walk conflict markers file-by-file, `git rebase --continue` after each. CLI reports, skill guides — the terse CLI warn line is enough for users running sdd alone, while agent-driven sessions now get a concrete path forward instead of a report-and-drop. Refines AC6 of the background-sync plan (d-tac-hsu) without changing its contract. Committed in 49f2e7c.
