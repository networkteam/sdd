Flow dialogue context into entries and pre-flight validation. Two items: (1) Update SKILL.md 'Always dialogue before capturing' section — add guidance to include substantive dialogue conclusions (trade-offs, alternatives rejected, reasoning) in the entry description at play-back. Additionally, when pre-flight rejects an entry, use the rejection as a prompt to check what dialogue reasoning wasn't captured in the entry text — revise the entry content, not just the wording. (2) Update pre-flight prompt templates — add instruction to treat dialogue-sourced reasoning in entry text as human-confirmed context. Focus validation on structural completeness (missing refs, uncovered requirements, no evidence), not re-evaluation of confirmed rationale.
