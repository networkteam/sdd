AI making code cheap shifts the bottleneck to direction — knowing what to build, why, and with what constraints — validating SDD's positioning as the infrastructure for exactly this layer. The emerging "dark factory" pattern (autonomous agents executing from a spec-and-constraints layer) points toward SDD's long-term role in multi-agent work.
