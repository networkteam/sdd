Post-session dialogue review as a structural mechanism for catching missed signals. Mining session JSONL for evaluation gaps, silent scoping decisions, and meta-level observations the agent failed to surface in the moment. Complements pre-flight gates: gates catch known failure modes before damage, dialogue review catches unanticipated ones retroactively. Applies to all implementation sessions. Especially valuable during the current bootstrapping phase (SDD developing SDD) where every framework failure is simultaneously a practical problem and a design input — the dialogue is unusually dense with framework-relevant signals.
