A --dry-run flag on sdd new would let users test whether an entry would pass pre-flight without creating the entry or committing. Useful for iterating on entry framing without polluting the graph, and for evaluating the pre-flight itself — running different drafts through to see what it catches (exactly the kind of testing needed to evaluate the entry_quality template changes). Current workflow forces a commit-to-test cycle for anyone wanting to check whether their wording would pass, which discourages experimentation. Pairs with --skip-preflight as complementary control: skip bypasses, dry-run previews.
