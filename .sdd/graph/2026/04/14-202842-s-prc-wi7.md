Pre-flight for closing actions validates against full plan prose instead of focused acceptance criteria, causing rejections for missing algorithmic details already in code. Observed on the d-tac-017 closing action: 4 rejections for dedup algorithm, output format spec, and per-direction depth — all present in the plan attachment and implementation. Plans should carry explicit acceptance criteria that scope what pre-flight validates on closing, what the implementing agent uses as a work checklist, and what deviations must explain.
