Reshape the type system from three types (signal, decision, action) to two types (signal, decision) with explicit kinds. Signal kinds: gap (default), fact, question, insight, done. Decision kinds: directive (default), contract, plan, task. Actions dissolve into signal kind 'done' — a done signal must reference or close at least one decision. The gap kind is the primary driver of the sculpting loop: the delta between actual and target state that triggers decisions and work. See [design](./12-114647-d-cpt-omm/two-type-design.md) for full rationale, functional mapping, and examples.
