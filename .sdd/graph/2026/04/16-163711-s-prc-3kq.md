Catch-up in a fresh session should not suggest continuing active WIP work by default — that work is most likely in flight in another session. Only surface WIP markers owned by the current participant if they appear stale (e.g. older than ~1 day), framed as 'this might need attention' rather than 'continue here.' Other participants' WIP markers are informational context, not action items.
