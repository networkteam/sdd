Commits to incorporating the aspiration decision kind — proposed in s-cpt-7we and applied to SDD's own direction in s-cpt-wiv — into the two-type redesign plan (d-cpt-omm) rather than shipping it as a separate incremental change. Rationale: d-cpt-omm hasn't been planned yet, so widening scope now is cheaper than two rounds of type-system churn; the combined plan also forces useful thinking — what "done" means for an aspiration (it doesn't close), whether aspirations require medium+ confidence, how they relate to `insight` signals that might argue for or against them. Detailed planning of d-cpt-omm deferred to a future session; this decision fixes the sequencing.
