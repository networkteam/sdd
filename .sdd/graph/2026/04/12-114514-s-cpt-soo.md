The three-type system (signal, decision, action) doesn't feel right. Signals carry five distinct things (gaps, facts, questions, insights, ideas) — all forced into 'observation suggesting a gap.' Decisions carry four (directions, tasks, constraints, plans) — the kind field already acknowledges this but only partially. Actions are coherent but may not need their own type — they're signals that work was done. This strain surfaced from gap analysis of 164 graph entries against the Kōgen Coffee story. The sculpting philosophy (actual/target delta as primary driver) has no explicit representation in the type system.
