Armin Ronacher's "The Friction Is Your Judgment" talk (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_Zcw_sVF6hU) articulates the coordination problem SDD addresses: in AI-augmented teams, creating entities (humans + agents) now outpace those who can carry responsibility, and reviews get skipped. SDD decouples accountability from co-presence — the graph preserves judgment artifacts so review and responsibility don't require being there. Sharper framing of SDD's value than "memory across sessions."
