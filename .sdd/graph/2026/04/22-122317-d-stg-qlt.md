Humans and AI agents working in parallel stay coherent with each other. Decisions, reasoning, and context flow across participants — whoever picks up the thread (human or agent, same session or weeks later) can engage at the edge of thinking, not rebuild from scratch.

What this aspiration pushes against:
- **Silos** — participants working without awareness of each other's context; the same question being answered multiple times in different corners
- **Handoff friction** — work passes between participants (human to agent, agent to human, agent to agent, session to session) but the reasoning behind it doesn't travel with it

Branches from the dialogue aspiration (`20260422-122136-d-stg-beb`): alignment happens *through* dialogue. Without dialogue shaping decisions, parallel work fragments; the graph is what makes dialogue portable across participants and time.
