`sdd show` currently displays an entry's frontmatter (ID, Type, Layer, Kind, Confidence, Who, Refs, Time, Derived status) followed directly by the body, omitting the stored summary entirely from the output. This makes summary-body drift hard to spot — diagnosing whether a summary diverges from its body requires parsing the raw entry file or cross-referencing the `sdd list` summary line against the `sdd show` body output. Adding the summary as a labeled section between the frontmatter and the body in `sdd show` would surface drift inline during normal review, directly supporting diagnosis of the broader summary drift signal (20260427-151153-s-cpt-tdp). Mechanical CLI change with high diagnostic value.
