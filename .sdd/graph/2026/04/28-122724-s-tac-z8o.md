Fresh clones of an SDD-instrumented repo have no automated setup path: `.sdd/config.local.yaml` is correctly gitignored (each contributor carries their own canonical), and skill installation may or may not be present depending on how the project tracks scope. The canonical answer is to make `sdd init` the readiness check — on any run, detect missing local participant, detect missing skills at the project's recorded scope, and prompt for whatever is absent, with a clear non-interactive error when stdin isn't a terminal. Skill guidance and README should direct new contributors to run `sdd init` immediately after cloning. Detecting missing skills depends on the install-scope record proposed in the companion scope-persistence gap.
