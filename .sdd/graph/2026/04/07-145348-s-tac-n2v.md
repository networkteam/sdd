Keyword search (grep) for graph entries misses conceptually related entries with different wording. This is a discovery problem, not just scaling. Example: searching 'explor' won't find a signal about 'richer back-and-forth' even though they're related. Semantic search with embeddings would enable finding entries by meaning. Until then, the workaround is listing everything in a forked skill and using the LLM context as a semantic matcher — works but expensive.
