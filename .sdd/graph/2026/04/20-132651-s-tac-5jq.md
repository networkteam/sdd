## Deviation — AC-section presence stays in LLM pre-flight

Pre-flight AC #1 as written in `d-cpt-e7i` includes "plan AC section presence" as a structural check. During implementation I drafted a `hasACSection` markdown parser in Go, then reverted after dialogue: the heading-match is brittle (caps variants, h3 vs h2, checkbox marker drift) and duplicates the AC-presence rule already in `decision_refs.tmpl`. A false-negative mechanical check would block legitimate captures; a false-positive would waste the LLM call anyway. The check stays in the LLM layer. Worth revisiting before session 2 when the template overhaul lands — the right framing there is whether the LLM check survives or is replaced by a stricter convention-enforcing parser.
