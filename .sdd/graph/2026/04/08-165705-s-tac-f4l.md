sdd new stores short ID suffixes in closes and refs fields (e.g. closes: [0un, k6r]) but the CLI cannot resolve short suffixes back to full IDs during graph queries. Closures captured today (oka closing 0un/k6r, k07 closing oka, 8g2 closing mqd) are all ineffective — the graph still shows these entries as open. The CLI needs to either expand short IDs to full IDs at write time, or resolve short suffixes at query time.
