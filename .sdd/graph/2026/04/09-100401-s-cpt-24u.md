Explore mode discussions produce rich design detail — trade-off analysis, examples, format specs — that doesn't compress well into a single decision description. Our WIP marker exploration generated substantial design content that we packed into the decision entry, but this strains the 'keep entries digestible' guideline. The attachment mechanism (s-cpt-pql) would be a natural fit: the decision captures the commitment, an attached document preserves the full design reasoning. Without attachments, information from the discussion gets lost or the entry becomes overloaded.
