The 'active decisions' heuristic is too aggressive: any decision referenced by another decision is treated as superseded. But refs can mean 'builds on' not just 'replaces'. The conceptual framework decision (d-cpt-o8t) disappeared from active because the tactical CLI decision references it. Need a way to distinguish superseding refs from dependency refs.
