The SDD graph recognizes exactly two entry types — signal and decision — each carrying an explicit kind. Signal kinds: gap, fact, question, insight, done (default gap). Decision kinds: directive, activity, plan, contract, aspiration (default directive). Plan decisions require a `## Acceptance criteria` section with at least one checklist item; done signals require at least one `closes` or `refs` link. Entries outside these two types are rejected after migration. This contract defines the target state toward which d-cpt-e7i migrates the existing graph and supersedes the three-type vocabulary implicit in earlier entries without touching d-cpt-e1i's immutability claim or d-cpt-o8t's other stipulations.
