Tests and documentation are no longer quality signals — agents produce them trivially. The new signal is proof of actual usage, and the SDD graph accumulates this naturally through done signals. Over time, the graph becomes an evaluation coverage map: decisions with no downstream done signals are dark spots where validation is absent.
